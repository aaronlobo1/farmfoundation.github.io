About Us

We recognize that sustainable development and economic, social and environmental protection are global imperatives that present both opportunities and risks for business.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Globally rising living standards and population growth are accelerating the demands on our finite natural resources, and corporate and consumer preferences are increasing the demand for sustainable solutions. We believe the basic goals of sustainability are critical to long-term economic development and environmental well-being.

WE INVEST WITH A FOCUS

The blockchain revolution is still in its infancy, yet even now the transition from offline businesses to the web is impacting almost every branch of industry. This development is spawning exciting new business models with substantial growth potential. Our focus lies on companies in the consumer food segment. Our key criteria: potential market leadership coupled with a desirable food product which can be scaled and exported easily.

WE FOSTER GROWTH

Supporting fast-growing companies is our passion. We see ourselves not only as investors, but also as dependable strategic and operational navigators, guiding our portfolio companies as they head for leadership in national and international markets. Our drive is centered on developing these companies in partnership with their founders while demonstrating sound judgment, enthusiasm and decisiveness en route.

WE BANK ON SUSTAINABILITY

Helping company founders evolve their businesses sustainably – that's what counts for us. We want to take a long-term approach: our goal is to establish successful business models and genuine brands, and to generate value. Values that last.

Why food?

Food is an essential part of people’s lives, and as such is much more than just a means of survival. It is also the main factor in how we view ourselves and others. It plays a big role in all social and political issues, and is a bastion of popular media. As the growth of middle class in developing countries surges we anticipate a change in consumer taste and a huge demand for quality food. Additionally, with many states in the USA warming up to the idea of legalizing marijuana we believe that the food sector is poised for rapid growth especially with cannabis edibles/beverages.

The Thinktank

The idea that being smart in one domain qualifies one to just dabble in another is dangerous. Hence the funds and foundation would be managed by professionals with a proven track record. Half of the directors for the Farm Token Foundation would be appointed by the Farm Group and the other half elected by the members of the Farm Token Community. New directors shall be approved by community consensus and voted by existing directors, and they will rotate every few years based on expertise needed during the next phase of growth.
